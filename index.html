⚽ How It Works
1
Set up the gameEnter how many players are joining, rename them, and set a buy-in amount per person. Hit Apply.
2
Understand the potsAll 48 World Cup teams are split into pots by FIFA ranking — Pot 1 has the best teams, the last pot has the weakest. Each player gets one team from every pot.
3
Do the drawIn the Draw tab, select a player and hit Spin. The wheel randomly assigns them a team from the current pot. Repeat for every player, working through all pots.
4
Bonus drawIf there are leftover teams, the wheel picks a lucky player at random — that player then chooses whichever bonus team they want. Repeats until all bonus teams are gone.
5
Earn points all tournament — your teams earn points throughout the World Cup for winning their group, advancing in the knockouts, and ultimately winning the whole thing. Most points at the end wins the prize pool. Check the Leaderboard tab to see standings in real time.
